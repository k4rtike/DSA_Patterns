The goal of Kadanes Algorithm is to find the best ending or result at the last of the array as suited per situation.

The Idea is : If your previous serial is powerful than you(in sense  it's helping you to get the best ending),
                Just team up with that.
            If you are more powerful than the previous serial(existing subarray), in sense the previous serial is spoiling
                and you alone can make it better, abandon the previous serial-> Be a lone wolf.

            And each time you get the best ending keep a remeberence of it (save the result).
